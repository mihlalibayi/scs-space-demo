Who We Are

Africa-first satellite intelligence

SCS Space is a geospatial intelligence company built for Africa, not adapted from elsewhere. We combine open satellite data from programmes like Digital Earth Africa, ESA Copernicus, and NASA Earthdata with analytical rigour to produce intelligence that matters on the ground.

Based in Cape Town, South Africa, we serve governments, NGOs, agricultural operators, and researchers who need to understand what is happening across the continent, from soil moisture in the Sahel to crop stress in the Great Rift Valley.

Our intelligence layers are built on validated methodologies from leading Earth observation programmes, with more datasets and modules coming online regularly.

Why this matters

A 38% rainfall deficit combined with moderate vegetation stress is an early signal of potential crop yield shortfalls, often invisible to on-the-ground observers until it is too late.

SCS Space delivers this intelligence weeks before field reports arrive, enabling governments, NGOs, and agricultural lenders to act early, not after the fact.

Every metric is derived from validated satellite datasets at spatial scales from national to farm level.
